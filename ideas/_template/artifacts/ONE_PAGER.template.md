# <Idea Name>
## One-Pager

### Summary
One sentence describing the problem, solution, and outcome.

---

### The Problem
- Who experiences it
- How often it occurs
- Why it is expensive, risky, or painful
- Why it hasn’t been solved well

---

### The Solution
- What it is
- What it is not
- How it works at a high level
- Why it’s meaningfully different

---

### Market
- Primary customer
- Buyer vs user (if different)
- Market size (rough, honest)
- Why this market buys solutions like this

---

### Competitive Landscape
- What exists today
- Why incumbents are weak / misaligned
- Why this is hard to copy

---

### IP & Defensibility
- What may be patentable
- Structural or architectural advantages
- Know-how barriers

---

### Revenue Model
- How money is made
- Who pays
- Why pricing is plausible

---

### Exit Thesis
- Likely acquirer categories
- Why they would care
- What problem this solves for them

---

### Current Status
- Stage (SEED / SHAPING / SCREENING / READY)
- What’s validated
- What remains unknown

---

### The Ask
What I’m looking for *now* (capital, intros, diligence, etc.)
