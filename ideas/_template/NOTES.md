# Notes (Brain Dump)

Goal: get the idea out without organizing it.

Prompt:
- What keeps bothering me about this?
- Where have I seen this problem in the wild?
- Who complains about this (explicitly or implicitly)?
- What would a dumb but effective solution look like?
- Why does this feel solvable by me?

Rules:
- Bullet points only
- No editing
- No structure
- No judgment

Timebox: 20-30 minutes max
